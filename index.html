Our Mission

An Ecosystem That Drives Social & Environmental Change

We are building a network of collection centers that allow collectors to exchange plastic waste for digital benefits, making it too valuable to discard.

1. Earth's Plastic Halted

Waste is collected before it reaches waterways, ensuring every kilogram collected does not contaminate our ocean ecosystems.

2. Empowering Lives

We provide income, education, and access to services for collectors, turning waste into economic opportunity.

3. Traceable Social Plastic

The collected plastic is processed into high-quality, traceable recycled raw materials for companies.
